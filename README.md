# Budget Management App

A personal finance tool that helps you take control of your money — all running locally on your machine.

## What It Does

- **Manage budgets** — Create budgets and track where your money goes across customizable categories (Food, Transport, Entertainment, and more).
- **Record expenses** — Log every purchase against a budget and category to see exactly how you spend.
- **Check exchange rates** — See what your budget is worth in any currency using live exchange rates.
- **Review history** — Browse and filter past expenses, or view spending summaries grouped by category or month.
- **Set limits** — Define weekly, monthly, or annual spending caps and get warned before you exceed them.
- **Simulate savings** — Project how much you'll save over time based on your current habits, or experiment with what-if scenarios.
- **Get optimization tips** — Receive concrete suggestions on where you could cut spending to save more.

## How It Works

Everything runs locally — no server, no cloud, no account. Your financial data stays on your computer. The app uses an embedded database and exposes a REST API that a React frontend will consume.
